Mohabishwer Mohajatra
(মহাবিশ্বের মহাযাত্রা; Translation: The Great Journey of The Universe)

In this book we create a "timeline" of all the important events in the universe. Our timeline starts with the Big Bang. Then we will see how many galaxies, planets and satellites originated and how the stars started giving light. We will unravel the mystery of the origin of life on earth. We will reveal the history of the evolution of multicellular organisms from ancient unicellular organisms. We will know the story of all the huge creatures that roamed the earth including dinosaurs. We will see the origin, history and future of a very intelligent species called Homo sapiens.
